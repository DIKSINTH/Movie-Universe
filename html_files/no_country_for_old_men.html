No Country for Old Men is a 2007 American neo-Western crime thriller film written, directed, produced and edited by Joel and Ethan Coen, based on Cormac McCarthy's 2005 novel of the same name.

STORY

Llewelyn Moss , a Vietnam War veteran and welder who stumbles upon a large sum of money in the desert; Anton Chigurh , a hitman who is sent to recover the money; and Ed Tom Bell , a sheriff investigating the crime. The film also stars Kelly Macdonald as Moss's wife, Carla Jean, and Woody Harrelson as Carson Wells, a bounty hunter seeking Moss and the return of the $2 million.


CRITICS

Critics praised the Coens' direction and screenplay and Bardem's performance. No Country for Old Men was considered one of the best films of 2007

BOX OFFICE

The film became a commercial success, grossing $171 million worldwide against the budget of $25 million.
